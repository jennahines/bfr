Bridging divides for better health

More positive outcomes, greater equity, fewer errors, and lower costs—these are among the known benefits of interprofessional practice, a specific form of collaboration that unites team members from two or more distinct disciplines. Practitioners play a key role in advancing the study and practice of interprofessional care at Ohio State—both as seasoned advisors to students, as well as developing learners themselves.

Our BuckIPE curriculum offers a multitude of opportunities for practitioners to participate in acute, ambulatory, rehabilitation, and community settings. Our goal is to learn to work more effectively together so we can solve healthcare’s most pressing problems—and elevate the health and equity within our communities.
